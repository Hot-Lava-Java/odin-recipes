Garlic Bread

Home

Prep Time - 30 mins / Cook Time - 10-30 mins / Serves 6

Description

A simple and rustic garlic bread baguette. See the ingredients list and directions to make this below:

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • 150g salted butter (room temp)
  • 3-4 large garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley

Step-By-Step Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 190C/170C Fan/Gas 5 or an air fryer to 180C. Use a bread knife to cut through the baguette every 2cm, leaving the slices attached at the base so the baguette stays in once piece.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the butter, garlic, Parmesan (if using) and parsley with a little freshly ground black pepper.
  3. Spread about 1 tsp of the garlic butter between each slice of the baguette, until all the mixture is finished.
  4. To cook in an oven, place the bread on a baking tray and cook on the middle shelf for 15–20 minutes or until the butter has melted and the middle of the bread is piping hot. To cook in an air fryer, cut the baguette into lengths that will fit into your air fryer, if necessary, then bake in a single layer for around 10–14 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
